If AI has felt “meh” lately, try this one thing

Tina HuangApr 25 2026
Open in Gmail

Hi Thomas! How's it going?? 😊

A lot of people try AI once or twice, get a generic answer, and quietly conclude it's not for them.

I get it.

The generic prompts give generic results.

What I’d actually suggest is trying something much more specific to your real life.

Not:

"Help me be more productive."

More like:

“I need to meet my friend in one location, then get to another place within three hours. We need a restaurant that works for someone vegan and someone who hates spicy food, and it has to be walkable from the second location.”

The more specific and logistically annoying the problem, the better AI handles it.

That's the experiment. Take the messy, constraint-heavy, multi-variable thing sitting on your plate today and let AI work through it.

Not to replace your judgement.

Just to see what it does with real-world complexity.

A lot of people find that’s the moment AI starts to feel much more useful.

Thanks friend,

Tina 🐙
