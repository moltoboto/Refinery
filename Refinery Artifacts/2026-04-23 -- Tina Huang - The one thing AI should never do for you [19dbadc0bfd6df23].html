The one thing AI should never do for you

Tina HuangApr 23 2026
Open in Gmail

Hi Thomas! How's it going?? 😊

Everyone talks about what to use AI for.

I’ve also been thinking about what not to use it for.

There are a few things I personally won't hand off to AI, no matter how capable it gets.

First, things I genuinely enjoy doing.

For example, I make content because I like making content. I’m happy to use AI for parts around the process, but the creative part itself is something I still want to own.

Second, anything that relies heavily on taste or judgement.

AI can help generate options, organize ideas, or speed things up. But deciding what feels right, what’s worth building, or what actually makes something good that still comes from you.

The more valuable part is often the judgment behind the output, not just the output itself.

Third, anything with a meaningful downside if it goes wrong.

I'm not letting AI invest my money. I'm not giving it access to my main email. If the cost of being wrong is significant, a human needs to be in the loop.

It can be a very powerful tool, as long as you stay in the driver’s seat.

Thanks friend,

Tina 🐙
