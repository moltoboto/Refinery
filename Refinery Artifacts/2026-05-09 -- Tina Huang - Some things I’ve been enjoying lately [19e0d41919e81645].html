Some things I’ve been enjoying lately

Tina HuangMay 9 2026
Open in Gmail

Hi Thomas! Happy weekend! 😊

Thought I’d do something a little different today and just share a few things that have been on my mind lately.

On the anime side… I’ve been watching this older series called Shiki.

It’s a psychological horror anime about vampires slowly taking over a small Japanese village. But what makes it interesting is the moral ambiguity. By the end, you’re genuinely not sure who the “bad guys” are.

It’s dark, slow-burning, and definitely not for everyone lol.

But if you’re into psychological horror, it’s worth watching.

And on the reading side… I’ve been spending a lot of time reading about the AI industry and where investment money is flowing right now.

Not because I’m some finance expert (I’m definitely not 😅), but because I think it’s interesting to see where people are actually placing bets.

A lot of AI conversations online are driven by hype.

But money tends to move a little more carefully.

So sometimes, following where investment is going tells you more about where the industry might be heading than whatever trend is loudest on that week.

Anyway, just wanted to share a little slice of what I’ve been thinking about outside of work lately.

If you’ve been watching or reading anything good recently, reply and let me know!

I’m always looking for recommendations 😊

Thanks friend,

Tina 🐙
