🗝️ Key trends

We’re entering the Clipping Economy.

In a world where attention is fragmented, long-form content (podcasts, interviews, webinars) is just raw material. Distribution is driven by moments, not episodes.

The highest-leverage marketer in 2027 won’t be the one who creates the most content - but the one who identifies the 47-second clip inside a 2-hour podcast that generates 10M views.

That skill isn’t editing - it’s pattern recognition:

  • emotional spikes

  • contrarian soundbites

  • algorithm-friendly pacing

We’ll likely see a new role emerge: Chief Clipping Officer.

Because in the new media landscape, discovery doesn’t come from publishing - it comes from precision extraction.

  • Resolve AI raised $40M Series A extension to automate software operations and incident management.
