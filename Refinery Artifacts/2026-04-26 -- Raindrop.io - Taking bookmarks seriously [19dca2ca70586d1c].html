So, you've been using Raindrop for a couple of days now, but have you already discovered all the features? There might be some you've missed out on.

Personalize your collections

Customize your collection by selecting a custom icon from a vast selection of predefined icons. Choose a layout that suits your content and, if desired, hide fields you don't want to see.

These customizations are applied on a per-collection basis, allowing you to personalize each collection to your liking.

Highlight & annotate the web

Found an interesting quote or code snippet? Simply select the text and click Save highlight from the context menu. You can assign a color and add a brief note to provide additional context.

Raindrop always at hand

In Chrome, Firefox, and Edge, you can access your bookmarks with just one click in a dedicated sidebar. What's even cooler is that you can drag and drop new content straight from a web page. All you need to do is install our browser extension.

Have a different browser? Don't worry. You can install our desktop app instead and get the same one-click access experience.

Find a bookmark in seconds

In Chrome, Firefox, Opera, and Edge, you can use the address bar to search for a Raindrop bookmark. Type rd followed by your query, navigate with the keyboard, and press enter to open the bookmark you need. It's that easy!

Raindrop is also available in Raycast and Alfred, two great launchers. Just type a special keyword and you can access all your bookmarks without ever taking your hands off the keyboard.


Premium benefits

  

Talk to your bookmarks

Ask questions and get answers grounded in your saved content. Use the built-in AI assistant to organize your library with natural language and rediscover things you forgot you saved.


  

Never lose a bookmark

Avoid the frustration of broken or restricted links when revisiting bookmarks. Unlimited forever copies are created automatically in Permanent Library so you can always return to them.

With Daily Backups, you can restore accidentally removed bookmarks or entire collections.


  

Build your private search engine

The entire content of webpages, EPUBs, and PDFs you've saved is searchable with Raindrop Pro.

Look beyond titles and tags. It's like having your personal Google!


  

Really read it later

Short on time to read the article? Just set a reminder, attach a note and move on with your day. When it's time, Raindrop wakes up with a gentle email and mobile notification and gets you back to where you left off.


  

Declutter your collection

On average, 10-15% of a user's bookmarks are duplicates, and one in ten bookmarks are no longer accessible online. Remove them and keep your collection clean and clutter-free with Raindrop Pro.


  

Support us

Raindrop doesn't display ads, track, or sell your data. The app's revenue comes solely from direct sales to users. Ultimately, those who purchase Raindrop Pro are the ones who support and sustain its existence!
